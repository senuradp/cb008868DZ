Meet Bon Bon The Happy Panda

Meet BON BON the happy panda , Bon Bon is the first giant panda born at the Dehiwala National Zoo who is 6 years old. Unlike other pandas Bon Bon is a panda who is very friendly with humans and he also is a panda who has a smile on its face all the time. Nevertheless, Bon Bon is very good in hugging people and also giving them high-fives. Bon Bon made his first public appearance once he was 5 months old. On his first birthday the panda was featured on a show in the National Geographic Channel as he was the first giant panda who was born in the Dehiwala Zoo. Before he was named Bon Bon the panda cub was famously referred to as the "smarties" beacause as soon as the cub was born, the zoo keeper portrayed him as being the size of 2 mini smarties boxes and the name turned out to be mainstream as soon as the media-men captured it and later the authorities came in to a conclusion of naming the panda cub as Bon Bon which means "Candy" in French.
